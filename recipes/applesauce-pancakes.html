Applesauce Pancakes

Description

Enjoy a soft fluffy gluten-free breakfast with these easy to make applesauce pancakes! Whip these up in just a few minutes with only three ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup + 1 tbsp ground oats or oat flour

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, egg, and applesauce into a mixing bowl.
  2. Whisk ingredients until they are combined (the batter will be slightly lumpy). Do not over mix.
  3. spray a large frying pan or griddle with cooking spray, or dampen a paper towl with vegetable oil and rub the top of the pan. Heat the pan over medium-low heat and add pancake batter using a 1/4 measuring cup. Fit as many that will fit in your pan or griddle.
  4. Cook for 1 - 2 minutes or until small bubbles begin to form on the surface, and the edges have set. Flip the pancake over and cook for another 1 - 2 minutes until the other side is also a golden brown.
  5. Remove from pan and enjoy. =)
